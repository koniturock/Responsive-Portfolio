About Me

I am an experienced OR nurse who studied Health Information Technology at a graduate level. I have over 15 years of experience in Operating room nursing working in cardiac specialty. I have a special interest in managing patient information and the wisdom to improve the data care for the facility. I offered the best possible education and support in the use of new technology for clinical staff. I am an excellent mentor with great abilities of training, educating and demonstrating clinical and informatics knowledge to new and current nursing staff. The specific EHR systems I have used are: Mediware, EPIC, VISTA, and CPRS.

I am a very versatile Circulating Nurse ensuring the safety of patients by serving as an integral member of the surgical team. I am adept at collaborating with key Operating Room personnel and rendering attentive preoperative and postoperative nursing care with a specialization in establishing compassionate relationships with patients through astute observation. I am an excellent trainer and have focused on data management within the healthcare database.
